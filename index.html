HighRoller

HighRoller was designed to help wealthy individuals find and acquire luxury forms of transportation with as much ease and expertise as possible. No other site on the market has the extensive collection or such a knowledgeable staff.

AFTER

AFTER is a non-profit website designed to help disseminate information immediately after a natural disaster. The application makes it easy for individuals to find out what relief organization are in the area and what services they provide. Disaster relief organizations can post and manage their events and all of their contact information.

GOODVIEWS

Have you seen ...? What do you think of...? Are these conversations you have had with any of your friends, coworkers, or family? If so, GoodViews is the site for you. GoodViews allows you to keep track of TV shows and movies in lists that you are watching, have watched or want to watch, and it allows you to view your friends lists of movies and Tv shows. You will now be able to contribute to the conversation about what everyone is watching because you are tracking with GoodViews!

GoodViews was created with the latest javascript technology. It uses angular on the front end with a Node.js backend and data that is safely stored in a mysql database.
